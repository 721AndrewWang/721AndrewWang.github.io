The Sandwich Technique in Psychology and Communication

The sandwich technique is also applied in communication and psychology, particularly in delivering feedback effectively. This method places constructive criticism between two positive statements, aiming to reduce defensiveness and increase receptivity.

Example in Feedback

"I appreciate your effort on this project. However, I noticed some inconsistencies in data analysis. I am confident you can refine this with minor adjustments."

Benefits

  • Encourages openness to criticism
  • Reduces defensiveness
  • Enhances motivation for improvement
